Corn Salad

Description:

Corn Salad with sweet peppers

  • 1 Sweet Corn Can
  • A mix of sweet peppers
  • PIckled cucumbers (optional for extra salty flavor)
  1. Drain can of corns
  2. Cut peppers
  3. add corn and peppers into a bowl
  4. add lemon juice and olive oil with a pinch of salt and stir
