Red Cabbage Salad

Description:

Red Cabbage Salad in lemon juice and olive oil

  • Red Cabbage
  • Lemon / Lemon Juice
  • Olive Oil
  • Garlic (Optional)
  • Salt
  1. Rinse Red Cabbage
  2. Cut into half, then quarters then slice across
  3. Put in bowl, mix with lemon juice and salt
  4. Leave in fridge for 3 days in a covered bowl until cabbage turns pink, mix once per day until 3rd day
  5. Serve
