Roasted eggplants

Description:

Roasted Eggplants with garlic powder and paprika

  • Salt
  • Eggplants
  • Garlic powder
  • Lemon Juice
  • Paprika
  1. Cut eggplants and sprinkle with salt to dry moisture, leave them out for 45 minutes
  2. Wipe moisture and waterdrops off eggplants
  3. Preheat Oven to 180 degrees
  4. Brush Oil over the dried eggplant
  5. Lie the eggplants down on the sheet or facing up if using an air fryer and put in the oven for 30 minutes on a heat of 200
