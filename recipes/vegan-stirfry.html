Vegan Stir Fry Noodles

Description:

Rice noodles cooked in soy sauce with veggies

  • Corn oil/Canola
  • Garlic
  • Onion
  • Sweet pepper preferably red and orange
  • Mushrooms
  • Hot pepper
  • Soy sauce for cooking
  • Tiriyaki sauce
  • Rice/Wheat noodles
  • Sesame Oil
  1. Preheat oil in frying pan
  2. Cook noodles in boiling water for 15 minutes in a pot
  3. Add the hard vegetables like Onions and carrots to the frying pan
  4. Add sweet pepper and turn up the heat
  5. A minute after add hot peppers and stir
  6. Add Mushrooms after 5 minutes and add a spoon of cooking soy sauce
  7. Make sure to check noodles so they don't overcook. turn off heat under the boiling pot and drain
  8. Add noodles to the frying pan after the veggies have all become tend
  9. Stir and add sesame oil and tiriyaki sauce
